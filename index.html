Meet the Founder

Dimitris Bisias

Founder & Head Coach | CORE X – Pilates. Movement. Fitness

Movement is not just exercise. It is the way the body gains strength, balance and quality of life.

Dimitris Bisias is the founder of CORE X – Pilates. Movement. Fitness, a boutique studio in Ano Patisia, created with the goal of offering a complete training experience through Pilates and functional training.

He is a graduate of Studio One – School of Fitness with specialized training in:

  • Group Exercise Leader
  • Advanced Choreographer
  • Personal Training
  • Pilates Reformer
  • Pilates Cadillac

With over ten years of experience as a fitness trainer and Pilates instructor, he discovered Pilates through his dedication to physical fitness and found not just a method, but his true calling. His approach to Pilates and strength training is built on power, balance and mindful movement, transforming the way his clients move, feel and live.

"Movement is the foundation. Strength is the result."
What We Offer

Our Services

Pilates Reformer

Strength • Control • Balance

Experience the transformative power of reformer Pilates. Our premium equipment and expert instructors guide you through precise movements that strengthen your core, improve flexibility, and enhance body awareness.

Strength Training

Power • Focus • Results

Build functional strength with our comprehensive training programs. From free weights to resistance machines, develop power and definition in a focused, supportive environment.

Cardio Zone

Endurance • Energy • Progress

Boost your cardiovascular fitness with our dedicated cardio zone. State-of-the-art treadmills, bikes, and rowing machines help you build endurance and track your progress.

Pilates Mat & Personal Training

Flexibility • Precision • Guidance

Discover the foundations of Pilates through mat-based sessions combined with one-on-one personal training. Tailored programs that focus on alignment, core stability, and mindful movement to help you reach your unique goals.
